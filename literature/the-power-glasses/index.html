    The Power Glasses

    “The Power Glasses” published in March 2024.

    In ‘The Power Glasses’, join a curious young girl on an imaginative adventure that unlocks the secrets of perception. Through her journey, readers will discover the transformative power of seeing the world through others’ eyes, emphasizing the importance of empathy and understanding. They’ll understand how small change in our perspective can resolve many arguments and improve communication. Perfect for all ages, this enchanting tale invites readers to embrace the magic of perspective and the wonders it reveals.
