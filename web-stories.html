げつ
(Getsu)

Getsu sounds as  Guest. Imagine your Guests arrived on the first day of the week (Monday) which is 2  days before the check-in.

Monday

か 
(ka)

"Ka" sounds like का (Hindi) which is 2nd word in the phrase we remembered. 

Tuesday

すい 
(Sui)

The suite booked is as dirty as their suitcase. Guests can check-in only on Wednesday.

Wednesday

Image Credit: Unsplash

もく 
(Moku)

Moku (मोकु) is similar to मुझे (Hindi). You, a manager, ask the staff on Thursday to clean the room.

Thursday

(Kin)

Kin sounds as clean. Your staff delayed the cleaning as Friday is the cleaning day.
